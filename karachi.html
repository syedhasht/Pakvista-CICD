Karachi - City of Lights

Karachi is Pakistan's largest city and a major coastal, commercial, cultural, and historical center. The city offers beautiful beaches, museums, architectural landmarks, shopping destinations, and a vibrant food culture that attracts visitors from across the country.

Clifton Beach

Clifton Beach is one of Karachi's most famous tourist attractions located along the Arabian Sea. Visitors enjoy camel riding, horse riding, family picnics, street food, and beautiful sunset views. The beach remains a popular recreational destination throughout the year.

Mohatta Palace

Mohatta Palace is a historic building constructed in 1927 and is considered one of Karachi's most important architectural landmarks. The palace now serves as a museum and cultural center where visitors can explore art exhibitions and historical displays.

Pakistan Maritime Museum

Pakistan Maritime Museum showcases the country's naval history and maritime heritage. The museum features naval equipment, historic vessels, a submarine, maritime artifacts, and educational exhibits for visitors of all ages.
