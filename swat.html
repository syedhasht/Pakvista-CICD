Swat - Switzerland of Pakistan

Swat Valley is famous for its green mountains, rivers, historical sites, winter tourism, markets, and peaceful natural scenery. It is among the most visited tourist regions in Pakistan.

Malam Jabba

Malam Jabba is Pakistan's most famous ski resort and one of the leading tourist destinations in Swat Valley. It offers skiing, chairlift rides, hiking opportunities, and breathtaking mountain views throughout the year.

Mingora Bazaar

Mingora Bazaar is the commercial center of Swat and a vibrant marketplace where visitors can purchase traditional handicrafts, gemstones, local products, clothing, and souvenirs.

Swat Museum

Swat Museum preserves the archaeological and cultural history of the Swat Valley. It contains important Buddhist relics, ancient sculptures, manuscripts, and historical artifacts that highlight the region's rich heritage.
